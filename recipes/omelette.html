Omelette Recipe


The recipe of this tasty looking omelette follows

This cheese omelette is great for breakfast. Nothing makes me happier. When cooking an omelette, it's important that you keep the temperature on the low side, as you don't want it to turn brown.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp of milk
  • white pepper
  • butter
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Mix everything
  2. Cook
  3. Enjoy your brekkie
